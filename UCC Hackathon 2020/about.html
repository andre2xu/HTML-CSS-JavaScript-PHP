What is a flashcard?

You may already know what a flashcard is from school but for those who don't, it is basically a card containing a small amount of information. It can be very helpful for when you are revising for an exam or if you want to improve your memory retention.



How to use our tool

The great thing about our tool is that it is very easy to use. To get started, the first thing you must do is navigate to our Flashcard Generator by clicking on Create at the top right of your screen, then you add a flashcard, put in what you want in the appropriate boxes then save it. You click once to either view or hide the hidden message, and if you want to delete the flashcard you double click on it - see very easy?



Flashcard or notebook?

Unfortunately due to the pandemic, some people may or may not have access to copies or notebooks because either their local stationery shops are closed or they cannot travel to them. That is not a problem though! Our online flashcard generating tool can serve as a notebook to store all the vital information you need and it is completely free!
